Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Duration: 30-45 minutes per session
Activities:
Walking: Engage in brisk walking sessions outdoors or on a treadmill to burn calories and improve cardiovascular health.
Swimming: Incorporate swimming sessions in a pool or aquatic center for a low-impact, full-body workout that burns calories and builds endurance.
Cycling: Take leisurely bike rides around your neighborhood or on a stationary bike to increase heart rate and burn calories.

Strength Training

Frequency: 2-3 times per week
Bodyweight Exercises: Perform basic bodyweight exercises such as squats, lunges, wall push-ups, and seated leg lifts to build muscle strength and improve metabolism. These exercises can be done at home without the need for any equipment.
Yoga or Pilates: Include yoga or Pilates sessions focusing on strength-building poses to improve flexibility, muscle tone, and overall body awareness. These exercises promote relaxation and stress reduction while aiding in weight loss. Resistance Band Workouts: Incorporate resistance band exercises such as bicep curls, shoulder presses, and leg lifts to add resistance and challenge to your strength training routine. Resistance bands are affordable, portable, and versatile, making them an excellent option for at-home workouts.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water before meals to help control hunger and avoid overeating. Aim for at least 8-10 cups of water per day.

Portion Control:

Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes and prevent overeating.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Activity Increase:

Incorporate more physical activities into daily routines, such as walking instead of driving or taking stairs instead of elevators.

Meal Planning:

Plan and prepare meals ahead of time to avoid unhealthy choices and ensure adherence to calorie goals.
