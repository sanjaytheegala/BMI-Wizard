Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 4-5 times per week
Duration: 45-60 minutes per session
Activities:
Brisk walking: Engage in brisk walking sessions outdoors or on a treadmill to burn calories and improve cardiovascular health.
Cycling: Take leisurely bike rides around your neighborhood or on a stationary bike to increase heart rate and burn calories.

Strength Training

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Activities:
Bodyweight exercises: Perform basic bodyweight exercises such as squats, lunges, wall push-ups, and seated leg lifts to build muscle strength and improve metabolism.
Resistance band workouts: Incorporate resistance band exercises such as bicep curls, shoulder presses, and leg lifts to add resistance and challenge to your strength training routine.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water before meals to help control hunger and avoid overeating. Aim for at least 8-10 cups of water per day.

Portion Control:

Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes and prevent overeating.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Activity Increase:

Incorporate more physical activities into daily routines, such as walking instead of driving or taking stairs instead of elevators.

Meal Planning:

Plan and prepare meals ahead of time to avoid unhealthy choices and ensure adherence to calorie goals.

Support System

Join a weight loss group or community for motivation and accountability. A support network can provide encouragement and help you stay on track.
