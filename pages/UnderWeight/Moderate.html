Meals And Timings

Early Morning (6:00 AM - 7:00 AM):

Meal: Warm milk with turmeric (haldi)
Benefits: Provides an initial source of calories and protein to kickstart your metabolism.
Ingredients: 1 cup milk, 1/2 tsp turmeric.

Breakfast (8:00 AM - 9:00 AM):

Option 1: Protein smoothie with banana and oats
Ingredients: 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup curd, 1 banana, 1/2 cup oats, 1 scoop protein powder.

Option 2:Paneer (cottage cheese) bhurji with whole-grain chapati
Ingredients: Paneer (100g), onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and spices. Side: Whole-grain chapati (2)

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM):

Option 1: Banana and a handful of almonds
Option 2: Boiled eggs (2) and a fruit (apple/orange)

Lunch (1:00 PM - 2:00 PM):

Option 1: Egg bhurji with whole-grain chapati and cucumber salad
Option 2: Chicken curry with brown rice and mixed vegetable sabzi

Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):

Option 1: Greek yogurt (plain or with honey) and a handful of walnuts
Option 2: Roasted chickpeas and an apple

Evening Snack (6:00 PM):

Option 1: Sprouts chaat
Option 2: Cheese and whole-grain crackers (1 cup)

Dinner (8:00 PM - 9:00 PM):

Option 1: Stir-fried paneer with vegetables and brown rice
Option 2: Grilled fish with millet roti and a side of mixed vegetable sabzi

Excercise Regimen

Strength Training

Frequency: 3-4 sessions per week
Rest: Allow at least one rest day between sessions
Duration: 30 minutes per session

Day 1: Upper Body:

Push-Ups: 4 sets of 12-15 reps
Dumbbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
Bent Over Rows: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2: Lower Body:

Squats: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 3: Rest or Light Activity:

Light cardio (e.g., brisk walking or swimming)
Stretching or foam rolling

Day 4: Full Body:

Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
Front Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 5: Rest or Light Activity:

Yoga or Pilates session to enhance flexibility and muscle recovery

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water throughout the day, aiming for at least 8 glasses. Include other hydrating options like coconut water or fresh fruit juices.

Meal Preparation:

Plan meals ahead of time to ensure balanced nutrition. Keep healthy snacks readily available for convenience.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Consistency:

Maintain regular eating and exercise habits to support overall health.Stick to a consistent meal and workout schedule.

Supplements:

Consider adding a protein powder to smoothies to help meet daily protein goals.Multivitamins and fish oil supplements can support overall health.

Mindset:

Stay patient and consistent; muscle gain takes time. Focus on gradual progress and celebrate small milestones.
