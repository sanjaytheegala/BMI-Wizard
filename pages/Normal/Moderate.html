Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Duration: 20-30 minutes per session

Activities:

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT): Intervals of intense exercise followed by short recovery periods to maximize calorie burn and cardiovascular fitness.

Strength Training

Frequency: 4-5 times per week
Activities:

Advanced Lifting Routines: Focus on compound movements like squats, lunges, push-ups, and pull-ups.
Progressive Overload: Continuously challenge muscles by gradually increasing repetitions, sets, or intensity of exercises over time.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water throughout the day, aiming for at least 8 glasses. Include homemade beverages like buttermilk or coconut water for hydration.

Meal Preparation:

Use portion control to manage calorie consumption and support muscle maintenance and growth.Invest in quality storage containers to maintain food freshness and portion control.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Consistency:

Maintain regular eating and exercise habits to support overall health.Stick to a consistent meal and workout schedule.

Track Progress:

Utilize wearable tech or simple logs to monitor activity levels, heart rate, and calorie expenditure during workouts. Keep a training log to track progress and identify areas for improvement.

Professional Guidance:

Regularly consult with fitness and nutrition experts to fine-tune training and dietary plans for optimal results. Consider working with a personal trainer to ensure proper form and technique during advanced lifting routines.
