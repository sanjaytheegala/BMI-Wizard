Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 3-5 times per week
Duration: 30 minutes per session

Activities:

Walking: Brisk walking outdoors or on a treadmill
Light Jogging: Jogging at a moderate pace to elevate heart rate
Swimming: Swimming laps or water aerobics for cardiovascular fitness.

Strength Training

Frequency: 2-3 times per week
Activities: Bodyweight exercises focusing on functional movements.

Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps (each leg)
Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, step forward with one leg, lower hips towards the floor, keeping front knee aligned with ankle, push back to starting position.

Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Instructions: Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, lower body towards the floor, keeping elbows close to body, push back to starting position.

Planks: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds
Instructions: Start in a push-up position with elbows bent at 90 degrees, maintain a straight line from head to heels, engage core muscles.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water throughout the day, aiming for at least 8 glasses. Include herbal teas or infused water for variety.

Meal Preparation:

Plan meals ahead of time to ensure balanced nutrition. Keep healthy snacks readily available for convenience.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Consistency:

Maintain regular eating and exercise habits to support overall health.Stick to a consistent meal and workout schedule.

Track Progress:

Maintain awareness of portion sizes and overall calorie intake. Listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.

Mindset:

Embrace simplicity in diet and exercise for long-term sustainability. Focus on overall health and well-being rather than strict calorie counting.
