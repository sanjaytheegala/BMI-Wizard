Excercise Regimen

Cardio

Frequency: 4-5 times per week
Duration: 45-60 minutes per session
Activities:
Brisk Walking: Walk at a moderate pace, focusing on increasing heart rate and calorie burn.
Elliptical Training: Use elliptical machines at the gym for low-impact cardio workouts.
Cycling: Ride a bicycle outdoors or use a stationary bike indoors for cardiovascular exercise.

Strength Training

Frequency: 3-4 times per week
Activities:
Resistance Training: Perform exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and rows with moderate weights to build muscle and boost metabolism.
Dumbbell Workouts: Incorporate dumbbell exercises like shoulder presses, bicep curls, tricep extensions, and chest presses to target specific muscle groups.
Yoga: Include yoga sessions focusing on strength-building poses such as plank, warrior poses, and chair pose to improve flexibility and muscle tone.

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water before meals to help manage hunger and prevent overeating.

Portion Control:

Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes and prevent overeating.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Track Progress:

Regularly assess weight, strength gains, and overall progress to make necessary adjustments to the diet and exercise routine.

Support System:

Engage with a support group or fitness community for motivation and accountability on your weight loss journey.

Activity Increase:

Incorporate more physical activities into daily routines, such as walking instead of driving or taking stairs instead of elevators.

Meal Planning:

Plan and prepare meals ahead of time to avoid unhealthy choices and ensure adherence to calorie goals.
