Excercise Regimen

Strength Training

Frequency: 2-3 times per week
Activities:

Bodyweight Exercises:

Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Planks: Hold for 30-60 seconds per set

Additional Tips

Hydration:

Drink water before meals to help manage hunger and prevent overeating.

Portion Control:

Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes and prevent overeating.

Sleep:

Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall well-being and recovery.

Track Progress:

Regularly assess weight, strength gains, and overall progress to make necessary adjustments to the diet and exercise routine.
